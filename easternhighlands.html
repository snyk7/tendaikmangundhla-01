Inyanga

Located in the northern part of the Eastern Highlands. In this area which is inside a national game park; Nyanga National Game park one will get to experience and enjoy the highest mountain in Zimbabwe, the sacred Mount Nyangani. Also in that area you can get to view the 2nd tallest waterfall in Africa, Mtarazi Falls and if you are feeling adventourous you can go for the skybridge across the Mtarazi Falls and/or speed across the falls on the highest Zipline in the world whilst overlooking the beautiful Honde Valley courtesy of Far & Zimbabwe

Places To Stay In Nyanga

  • Troutback Inn
  • Mont Claire Hotel

Vumba

Located in the middle of the eastern Highlands is the evergreen, picturesque Vumba and Burma Valley. Not many roads in the world will take your breathe away more than the Vumba Burma Valley route. The air is so fresh, unpolluted, naturaly scented and purified. At some points on the route you will be driving input a tunnel of tree canopies and vegetation.

Places To Stay In Vumba

  • Leopard Rock Hotel
  • White Horse Inn

Chimanimani

Located in the southern art of the eastern highlands this mountainous place is a haven for bird lovers and some very rare flowers. On your way to Chimanimani you will get to enjoy the Hot Springs which are located just before you get deep into Chimanimani. Dont forget your camera because you gonna want to snap a load of pictures.

Places To Stay In Chimanimani

  • Frog & Fern
  • Heaven Lodge

Mutare

Just before you get to Vumba you will pass city of Mutare "Valley of Kings and Queens". Just like the other attractions Mutare is perfectly located and iframe you visit using the Harare road the Christmass Pass will deliver the best entrance that you can ask for into any City or Town in Zimbabwe and you will understand why it was named the Valley Of Kings and Queens
